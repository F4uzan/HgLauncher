HgLauncher is a launcher with a design philosophy of 'if it works, it works.' Consisting of simply a scrolling list of apps, there is very little visual cue to disturb you. If all you want in a launcher is for it to just launch apps, then you may be looking at the right launcher.

Features:
• Simple and quick; no one likes waiting.
• Unobtrusive user interface: show your wallpaper in all its glory!
• Small but extensive user interface customization.
• Support for all kinds of custom launcher features: icon packs, app hiding, and more!
• Minimal memory usage while preserving the best performance.
• Completely FLOSS; no ads, no feature restrictions, no silly nags.
• …And more to come!
